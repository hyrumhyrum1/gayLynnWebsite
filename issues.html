Securing our Future

Utahns are no strangers to struggle. Our heritage is one of hard work, perserverance, and determination. Presently, economic turmoil threatens to diminish the hard work of many Utah families. Wage stagnation, automation and outsourcing are exacerbating economic inequity like never before. Gay Lynn is committed to working with experts and community advocates to find solutions that will protect our work and secure our future.

Providing Quality Education

Especially in these difficult times, we believe that quality public education is among the most important services provided by the state. As your representative, Gay Lynn will fight to protect teachers, students, and schools. Together, we can ensure equal access to quality education for all.

Addressing Healthcare Disparities

The global COVID-19 pandemic has shown us that access to affordable healthcare must be improved. Quarentine has worked in conjunction with our propensity towards addiction to create an environment that begs for expanded access to mental and emotional health services. GayLynn understands the difficulty that accompanies poor mental and physical health and is committed to listening to experts and making the best choices for all Utahns.

Investing in Housing

Utah's rising home costs are driving away young families and talented youth. To stay in the state, our children are being forced to either take on prohibitively expensive mortgages or rent indefinately. It's time for Utah to invest in more affordable housing.
